## 5. Why dual licensing?

Open-source agricultural traceability infrastructure should be a public good. The AGPL-3.0 ensures it stays that way — improvements made by anyone running it as a service must flow back to the community.

At the same time, sustaining the project long-term requires revenue. Commercial licenses fund full-time maintainers, security audits, EUDR regulatory tracking, and Hedera testnet/mainnet operations.

The two work together: every commercial customer underwrites the open-source version that everyone else uses for free.
