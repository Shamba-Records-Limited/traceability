# Authors

Shamba Traceability is built by Shamba Records Limited and a growing community of contributors. Every accepted pull request adds to this list; the canonical record is the `git log` of this repository.

## Maintainers

See [`MAINTAINERS.md`](./MAINTAINERS.md) for the current list of maintainers and their areas of responsibility.

## Contributors

A complete, automatically-updated list is available on GitHub: <https://github.com/Shamba-Records-Limited/traceability/graphs/contributors>.

If you would like to be acknowledged here under a name or handle different from the one in your commits, open a PR editing this file.

- The [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) for the basis of our Code of Conduct.

- The [Decentralized Identity Foundation](https://identity.foundation) and the W3C Verifiable Credentials Working Group.
- Cooperatives, farmer organisations, exporters, and field auditors across East Africa whose feedback shapes the product.
